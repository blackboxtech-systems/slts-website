The Workflow

Violation Detection

The violence detection system uses advanced AI algorithms to monitor traffic light intersections for any signs of violence or criminal activity. This technology ensures real-time detection and immediate action to maintain public safety.

Data Transmission

Data transmission is a critical component that ensures all collected data from traffic lights, sensors, and cameras is securely and efficiently sent to the central monitoring system.

Offense Registration

The offense registration system records all traffic violations detected by the traffic light management system, creating a comprehensive database for law enforcement and legal purposes.

Automated Notification
