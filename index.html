What is SLTS

The SLTS is an intelligent and intuitive platform that provides a robust traffic management system using AI and big data to identify traffic offences and alert the relevant authorities using a crowd-sourcing model.

The Strict Liability Traffic Systems (SLTS) is a cutting-edge platform designed to enhance traffic management, enforce road safety laws, and reduce road accidents. By utilizing artificial intelligence (AI) and big data, SLTS can identify traffic offences, accidents, and other incidents in real time, providing valuable evidence for law enforcement agencies, government bodies, and security services, our aim is to;

  • Reduce road traffic accidents significantly.
  • Fight crimes and enforce laws 24 hours, every day.
  • Be the model law enforcement body in Nigeria
  • Generate revenue for the country.

Frequently Asked Questions

These FAQs address common concerns and provide clear, concise information to help users understand the services and systems offered by SLTS.

1. What technology do you use to detect traffic violations?

Our system employs advanced AI and machine learning algorithms integrated with high-definition cameras and sensors to detect traffic violations such as running red lights, speeding, and illegal turns. This technology ensures accurate and reliable detection, reducing human error and enhancing traffic law enforcement.

2. How does the violence detection system work?

Our violence detection system utilizes AI-powered monitoring to analyze real-time video feeds from surveillance cameras. It identifies suspicious activities and potential violent behavior, sending immediate alerts to authorities for swift intervention. This proactive approach enhances public safety at traffic intersections and surrounding areas.

3. Can I appeal a traffic violation ticket issued by your system?

Yes, you can appeal a traffic violation ticket through our online appeals portal. Simply log in, provide the necessary details and any supporting evidence, and submit your appeal. You will be able to track the status of your appeal in real-time, ensuring a transparent and fair review process.

4. How secure is the data transmission in your system?

Data security is a top priority for us. Our system employs robust encryption protocols to ensure that all data transmitted between traffic lights, sensors, cameras, and our central monitoring system is secure. We also implement redundancy measures to prevent data loss and maintain the integrity of our data transmission.

5. What payment options are available for settling traffic fines?

We offer multiple payment options for your convenience. You can pay your traffic fines using credit/debit cards, bank transfers, or digital wallets through our secure online payment portal. Upon successful payment, you will receive an instant confirmation and an electronic receipt for your records.
